A modern studio apartment available right next to the Linnanmaa university campus. In addition to schools, the nearby area features the Technology Village business cluster, Prisma hypermarket, and excellent sports facilities.

The windows, light surface materials, and furnishings create a sense of spaciousness and a cozy atmosphere. The floor is parquet, walls are light, and the bathroom is tiled. The apartment is located on the fourth floor. Despite its size, the kitchen is fully equipped, and the bathroom has space for a washing machine.

Buses run from Linnanmaa to the city center multiple times per hour, making the comprehensive services of the city center easily accessible. In addition to public transport, getting around by bike is also convenient.

In summary, Linnanmaa offers comfortable and peaceful living with everyday convenience.

The apartment is rented with an open-ended lease agreement (1 month notice period, no fixed-term contracts). Credit checks will be performed.

The rent includes heating and free 50M broadband (Elisa). The internet speed can be upgraded for an additional fee if needed.

A carport parking space is also available for rent at 40 €/month if needed.
